## INTRODUCTION

The importance of e-commerce in today’ s world cannot be overemphasized since it
provides an efficient means through which companies can sell their products and
services via digital media. The increasing use of the internet and smartphones has
provided many opportunities for companies operating in Bhutan to utilize the technology
and improve the efficiency of their operations. An example of such a company is ZheyGo
Bhutan,which sells fooditemsand connects customers torestaurants via amobile app.

The operations of ZheyGo are similar to other global companies, such as DoorDash and
Zomato, where the emphasis is on convenience, speed, and accessibility. Currently, the
website of ZheyGo serves only a promotional purpose and does not allow for any
e-commerce operations. Users are encouraged to download the company’ s mobile
applicationinsteadof making orders online.

This project will focus on the analysis of the company's business model, identification of
major problems with the current digital presence and development of an alternative
e-commercewebsitedesign.

## BACKGROUNDOF THECOMPANY / PROBLEMIDENTIFICATION.......................................

ZheyGo Bhutan is a digital service provider in the field of food delivery. The company
cooperates with local restaurants and offers delivery services to its clients in major cities
like Thimphu. The main service offered by ZheyGo is food ordering using a mobile
application that has gained popularity among students and working people. The target
customers ofZheyGoarecustomersseeking quick andconvenientways to orderfood. As
the demand for online services increases, ZheyGo becomes a significant player in the
developing e-commerceof Bhutan.


However, there are a number of issues associated with running the company. These
include:

```
1. Firstly, the company's website does not provide the complete functionality of an
e-commerce site. Users visiting the site will not be able to place their orders;
hence,thewebsite is notused asan effectivedigital tool.
2. The company is highly dependent on the mobile application. In order to use the
services providedbyZheyGo,theusershould download the application first.
3. Few options of accessibility are available for users with low storage capacity or
poor internetconnection.
4. The site is inadequate when it comes to facilitating customer interaction. The site
simply offers basic information and download links for apps but fails to provide
customers with an interactive environment, like menu browsing and restaurant
choices.
```
Thisshowstheneedforan advancede-commercewebsite.

## MAPPING THEISSUE&SOLUTION............................................................................................

Thesuggestedwebsitedesign tackles thementioned business problems.

The problem of a lack of website functionality is solved through creating a more
interactive website that gives detailed information on the service. Although the
transaction takes place via the mobile application, the website can provide sufficient
information fora user.

The dependence on the mobile application is minimized with the help of the alternative
access point for the potential customer. With the help of a website, the user is able to
become interestedin aserviceand,consequently,download the app.


The problem of accessibility is solved by creating a light and responsive website that is
easy touseregardless,of the deviceused.

Customer engagement is made better with good design, efficient navigation, and useful
information. Restaurant showcases and “How It Works” pages increase the user
friendliness of thewebsite.

In terms of business, the website increases brand visibility and improves marketing
efforts. It makes ZheyGo competitive in the online environment with a professional
image.
